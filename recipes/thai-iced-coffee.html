Thai Iced Coffee

Description

Thai Iced Coffee is a refreshing and flavorful beverage made with strong brewed coffee, sweetened condensed milk, and a touch of cardamom. Served over ice, it offers a perfect balance of sweetness and bold coffee flavor, making it a popular choice for coffee lovers.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of milk
  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1/8 teaspoon of cardamom
  • 2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk
  • Ice (as needed)
