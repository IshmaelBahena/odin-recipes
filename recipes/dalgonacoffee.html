Dalgona Coffee

Description

Dalgona coffee is a whipped coffee drink made with instant coffee, sugar, hot water, and milk. It became popular during the COVID-19 pandemic as a fun and easy way to make a fancy coffee drink at home. The coffee mixture is whipped until it becomes light and frothy, then spooned over a glass of milk, creating a layered effect. The result is a creamy, sweet, and visually appealing beverage.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup whole milk
